Design Principles

Understand the intent
before defining the solution

Every feature has a business purpose. Strong designers understand why a product exists before deciding how it should work — preventing teams from optimizing the wrong things.

Design for today,
anticipate tomorrow

Strong design balances current resources with future needs. When they don't align, recognizing risks early reduces rework and supports sustainable growth.

Balance business goals
with user needs

User needs and business goals often align — but when they don't, thoughtful trade-offs matter. Strong product decisions prioritize long-term value over short-term convenience.
